Warwickshire Gamers

Board/war/miniature/video gamers and hobbyists in Warwickshire UK. A friendly bunch of grizzled wargamers and fresh recruits, Historical, Sci-Fi or Fantasy you will find we play it! Come say hi!

What We Play

Everything! We can't really give you a definitive list but here's some popular ones: Warhammer 40K, Age of Sigmar, Bolt Action, Necromunda, Middle Earth Strategy Battlegame (MESBG), Star Wars Legion, Killteam, Blitzkreig Commander, Dungeons and Dragons (D&D5E), SAGA, Ronin, Dead Mans Hand, Infinity, Warmaster, Warhammer Fantasy Battle (WHFB), Star Wars X-Wing, Star Wars Armada, Cursed City, Star Wars Imperial Assault, Lord of the Rings Journies in Middle Earth, Lord of the Rings LCG, Any Board Game.

When & Where?

We meet Mondays, 7.30pm at Lillington Club, Leamington Spa, there's free parking, a bar, and your first night's FREE!
